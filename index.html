About CT VIP & this data-viz

New Haven's Community-based organization for violence prevention.

CTVIP is a community-based program aimed at reducing youth violence in the greater New Haven area. CTVIP outreach workers with social rapport and     respect in New Haven maintain contact with individuals in the community, and stay informed about various social groups. This is a dataviz exploration of violence trends indicated by homicide rates. You can explore homicides trends at the national level, the state level, and county level. You can even search for your own county to see its trends over 2010-2019!
